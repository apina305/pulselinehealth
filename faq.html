Medical Coding Platform

The platform uses AI to analyze clinical documentation and suggest appropriate medical billing codes (ICD-10, CPT, HCPCS, modifiers). Simply enter notes or upload a document to get comprehensive coding suggestions.

PDF, JPG/JPEG, PNG, and TXT files up to 10MB. The platform processes documents client-side for maximum security.

The platform is designed for healthcare professionals including physicians, nurse practitioners, and certified coders. All code suggestions must be reviewed by qualified personnel before submission to payers.

Security & Compliance

Yes. We use client-side processing (data analyzed in your browser), TLS 1.3 encryption, automated PHI detection, and comprehensive security measures. You must de-identify all PHI before using the platform.

We store only account information (name, email, organization). We do NOT store clinical documentation, medical notes, patient information, or PHI. All clinical processing happens in your browser.
