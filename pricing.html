Why Choose Percentage-Based Billing?

Unlike fixed-fee billing services, our percentage-based model aligns our success with yours:

  • No Collections, No Fees: You only pay when we successfully collect payments
  • Motivated Performance: Our revenue is directly tied to maximizing your collections
  • Predictable Costs: Percentage-based fees scale with your revenue
  • No Hidden Fees: Transparent pricing with no surprise charges
  • Scalable Solution: Grows with your practice without renegotiation
